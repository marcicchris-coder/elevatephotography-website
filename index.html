  4. 4. Delivery: Receive MLS-ready files plus social-ready exports.

Listing Prep Checklist

A simple prep list helps your property look its best on shoot day.

  • Turn on all interior and exterior lights; replace mismatched bulbs.
  • Open blinds/curtains to maximize natural light and outside views.
  • Remove personal items and visible clutter from counters and tables.
  • Stage key rooms: living room, kitchen, primary bedroom, and bathrooms.
  • Park vehicles away from the front elevation and driveway where possible.
  • Secure pets and clear walkways for safe, efficient interior and drone capture.

About Chris

From the beginning of his career, he recognized the value of strong imagery in driving buyer interest and chose to personally manage the photography for each of his listings to ensure consistent, professional results.

Prior to his work in real estate, Chris gained experience in aerial photography while working for an aerial advertising company. This early exposure to capturing visuals from above sparked a long-standing interest in drone photography. After acquiring his first drone over a decade ago, he has continued to build his skills in both aerial and ground-based photography, combining creativity with technical precision.

By bringing together his knowledge of the real estate market and a passion for impactful visual storytelling, Chris helps agents and property owners showcase their listings in a way that stands out in a competitive market.
